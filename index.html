WHO WE ARE?

Meta Foxonary - innovative NFT organisation based on extending DAO E-democracy model.

Your fox doubles as your Meta Foxonary longe membership card, and grants you a voting power in all project key decisions.

Future project development and all project incomes like secondary market fees will be under control of project government which will be elected by holders voting once per month.

OUR MISSION

What comes to mind first when you think about something reliable? In our case, it's decentralization.

Decentralization - it's why crypto is better then fiat, why DAO is better then your angry boss and the reason to any banker to be pissed of.

But any system requires a bit of centralization, so our mission is to build project taking the best from the both worlds.

THE GRAND LODGE

When you buy a Fox, you aren’t simply buying an ordinary avatar or even a piece of art. You are gaining membership access to the Grand Lodge with the all holder-only benefits. Your Fox represents your digital identity and opens digital doors for you.

Every Fox holder, which we call Foxon, will have a voting-power proportional to the amount of NFTs on the wallet. You can use this voting power in order to participate in all key project decisions such as the election of the government, use of the budget and etc. Every Foxon can create their own team or join an existing one to participate in the government elections. You can find out more info about applying to elections on our discord server.

REDUCING SUPPLY, INCREASING VALUE

All Foxons are important in the Lodge but some of them are more important due to their unique talents. Many of them can see what others can't and should use this unique knowledge for the good of the Lodge. Other Foxons respect them and call them Wadjets. But there is a way to gain such power that even Wadjets can't imagine..

3 Wadjets can come together to merge themselves into Magister - the highest stage of Foxon development. Because of their power, magisters are the most respected Foxons in the lodge, and thats why they have additional benefits such as extra votes.
